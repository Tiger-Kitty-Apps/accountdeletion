Learn About Bitcoin

Bitcoin, created in 2009, is the first decentralized cryptocurrency. It was invented by an unknown person or group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto. Bitcoin transactions are verified by network nodes through cryptography and recorded in a public distributed ledger called a blockchain.

Interesting Facts:

  • Bitcoin has a limited supply of 21 million coins.
  • The first Bitcoin transaction was for two pizzas, costing 10,000 BTC.
  • Bitcoin mining consumes more energy than some countries.
